About

M.A Rabbit Farm is a pioneering private institution located in Kabustye cell, Masheruka parish, Sheema district, with a commitment to enhancing the livelihoods of Ugandans through sustainable rabbit farming. Specializing in the breeding of premium exotic rabbit varieties, our farm serves as a reliable source for the local community to obtain rabbits for both breeding and meat consumption.

Inquiries / Purchases

M.A Rabbit Farm

At M.A Rabbit Farm, our mission is to become Uganda's leading indigenous private institution dedicated to combating poverty and unemployment. Strategically located in Kabustye cell, Masheruka parish, Sheema district, our farm focuses on rearing high-quality exotic rabbit breeds. Our aim is to provide job opportunities for the local unemployed youth and offer a reliable source of rabbits for breeding and meat purposes to the community.

In addition to our core farming operations, we are dedicated to empowering the community by providing free training services at our on-site educational centers. These training sessions equip individuals with essential rabbit rearing skills, enabling them to contribute positively to the local economy.

Join us as we strive to create a lasting impact on the lives of Ugandans by fostering sustainable rabbit farming practices and cultivating new opportunities for growth and prosperity. Together, we can nurture a thriving community for generations to come.
